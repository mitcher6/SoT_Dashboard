Magaambya Campus Locations

Academic & Research Facilities

Archhorn Library Research

Enormous library containing thousands of years of published knowledge. Contains copies of all student/faculty works. Great for Gathering Information or Learning Knowledge.

Heron Archives Restricted

NPC: Takulu Ot (Office)

Contains records of unpublished research going back thousands of years. Contains dangerous/forbidden material. PCs can Earn Income (Arcana) assisting with research or learn new spells.

Elephant Museum Artifacts

Houses historical and magical artifacts. Dangerous artifacts are kept in the basement. Features a lecture hall where experts/expert students can Earn Income/Work using various Lores.

The Circle (U) Classroom

The first structure built at the academy. Features a grand glass dome over an ever-shifting mosaic map tracking Mwangi Expanse weather, locations, and events in real-time.

Student Life & Social Hubs

Spire Dormitory Housing

The PCs' current residence. A safe space to bathe and socialize with peers.

Dining Hall Social

NPC: Lumusi Yao (Kitchen)

Site of the cafeteria and meeting place for the Recreation Club. Mechanically, PCs can eat "Good Quality" meals here once per day at no cost or time investment.

The Magaambya Playhouse Clubs

Massive culture hub branching into dozens of rooms (some sealed for decades). Used by Expression, Explorer's, and Theater Clubs, or for privacy.

Shifting Greenhouse Nature

Packed with non-utility vegetation and nature spirits. Features bioluminescent plants at night; a highly popular dating site despite spirit mischief.

Nantambu Points of Interest

The Root & Alembic Alchemical Shop

Proprietor: Maela

Unassuming dark wood exterior covered in vines near a canal. Offers specialized jungle antidotes, enhanced elixirs (beyond Powderpile's Level 3 cap), and custom brews. Prices are high but she accepts trades for rare components or natural lore.

The Whispering Pages Bookstore

Proprietor: Old Man Jomo

Tucked in a quiet alley. Stock includes local lore, travelogues, annotated textbooks, and a discreet section of volatile/dangerous texts. Open to trading for unique stories.

The Ivory Compass Gambling House

Cool, fragrant environment with a central intricately carved table mapping the city's canals. Small magical boats track live flat-boat races for betting. Managed by a cold tiefling clerk.

Weekly & Downtime Systems

Weekly Activities

Activity Mechanics & Mechanics
Study Choose a Primary/Secondary branch. Attempt 2 skill checks using branch skills against standard DC. Select a teacher for review.
• Crit Success: +2 Study Points
• Success: +1 Study Point
• Failure/Crit Fail: 0 Points. Note: Requires daily DC 8 Flat check to avoid Fatigue if not resting.
Work Requires a job. Roll appropriate check (Earn Income). Increase success degree by 1 step and earn a full week of income.
Support Aid a cohort member making a Weekly check. If you Fail or Critically Fail your Aid check, it automatically upgrades to a Success.
Downtime Clear your entire weekly schedule to gain 4 days of flexible Downtime Activities.

Daily / Flexible Downtime Activities

Hang Out

Attend scheduled social engagements with NPCs. Missing a promised meetup negatively impacts relationships with them and their circle. Boost impact by bringing preferred gifts/companions.

Explore

Search Magaambya or Nantambu for a specific facility. Roll Diplomacy, Perception, or Lore. (DC -5 for generic facilities). Crit failure causes you to get lost and forces daily DC 8 Flat checks vs Fatigue.

Rest

Spend 2 Downtime days to clear all non-magical, non-beneficial conditions/diseases automatically. Clears fatigue and grants a +1 Status Bonus (+2 if healthy) to next week's checks.

⚠️ Running Fatigue Rule: Characters who fail study/exploration conditions must roll a DC 8 Flat Check at the start of every adventuring day to avoid becoming Fatigued until a formal Rest activity is taken.

Magaambya Faculty & Staff

Teacher Takulu Ot Cascade Bearers

Role: Mathematics & Orientation Mentor

An exceptionally kind, even-tempered human lore-speaker who dedicates his research to the art of teaching itself[cite: 1245, 1268]. He conducts the initial student group interviews and provides a supportive, stabilizing presence for incoming initiates[cite: 1234, 1255, 1261]. He almost always carries a wooden staff and a small anteater trinket[cite: 1259].

Teacher Koride Ulawa Rain-Scribes

Role: Magical Ecology & Entomology

The academy's premier insect and fungus expert, deeply fascinated by aspects of nature that others find repulsive[cite: 2087]. While undeniably brilliant, she is highly irresponsible—frequently skipping her own classes, ignoring safety rules, and indulging her favorite students[cite: 2095, 2096, 2097].

Teacher Zuma Emerald Boughs

Role: Defensive Magic & Rituals

An eccentric male half-orc conspiracy theorist with a razor-sharp mastery of defensive magic[cite: 140, 2324, 2326]. His classes rapidly pivot between brilliant breakdowns of useful rituals and nonsensical ramblings[cite: 2328]. He routinely recruits initiates to test high-concept experiments, like essence-sharing tattoos[cite: 2332, 2345, 2347].

Teacher Mafika Ayuwari Tempest-Sun Mages

Role: Martial Arts & Combat Training

A martially minded human celebrity who recently earned campus fame competing in Tian Xia's fabled Ruby Phoenix Tournament[cite: 2377, 2378, 2379]. Firmly believing that magical prowess must be paired with physical combat readiness, he hosts monthly sparring tournaments at the Leshy Gardens using tough, living-wood constructs[cite: 2377, 2380, 2381].
